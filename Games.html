Games

The Kirby series has a lot of games, which include 12 mainline games as well as 20 spin-offs, and 4 remakes too. Kirby's also featured in many crossover games, including Super Smash Bros., which is a giant fighting game crossover.

The foundation of each game is simple: beat enemies by copying their abilities. However, the charm of the franchise is the level design and the twists that are discovered in the gameplay.
