About Kirby!

Kirby is a pink puffball and the main character of the respective series. He is one of Nintendo's most famous and familiar icons. His round appearance, simple design and ability to copy his foes' powers have made him a well-known figure in videogames, which makes him one of the most iconic videogame characters.

He has a wide variety of copy abilities, ranging from simple abilities like Fire and Sword to quite complex powers like UFO. In some of the games, he also has access to unique Super Abilities, which include Ultra Abilities and Hypernova.

Ultimately, he is the epitomy of kindness as he has zero intent of malice and evil, all his intentions are towards the greater good, even his opposite self(AKA Shadow Kirby) is as pure as he is. He may also have the privilege of being the mightiest character in videogames, and even fiction! He also has different comics and even an anime after him.
