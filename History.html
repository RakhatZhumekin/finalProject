History

Kirby first debuted in 1992 in Kirby's Dreamland on the Gameboy, and he was created by game designer Masahiro Sakurai From there on, the franchise continued to have more games, spin-offs, and anime. Each entry in the series further explores the vast world of Pop Star, Kirby's home planet, and the Galaxy. In each game, Kirby clashes against a supreme entity, but thanks to his abilities and friends, he's always able to destroy them.

Each game introduced new characters. The first game only showcased King DeDeDe, Kirby's main foe, who later becomes his trusted ally. Later on, we get introduced to Meta Knight, a mysterious being who seems to be of the same species as Kirby. Of course, more formidable bosses approach as well. The stakes of each Kirby game is high, as it's in Kirby's hands to save the world and even the Universe. The lore of the games, despite the mascot being adorable and positive, is quite deep and extremely vast. Talking about here would take hours, if not days, so it's better to experience it for yourself if you're interested!
